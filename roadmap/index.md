## :material-strategy: How to Use Roadmaps

!!! tip "Roadmap Strategy"
    1. **Pick one primary roadmap** aligned with your career goal
    2. **Assess your current level** — identify what you already know
    3. **Follow the sequence** — each step builds on the previous
    4. **Complete courses** — use the learning portal courses for each topic
    5. **Build projects** — apply knowledge to real-world scenarios
    6. **Prepare for interviews** — use interview prep for your target role
