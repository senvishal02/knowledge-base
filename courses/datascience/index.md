### 🚲 Project 2: Bike Sharing Demand Analysis

#### Objective

Predict the **number of bike trips in a particular hour** based on environmental and seasonal factors.

---

#### Problem Statement

Lyft operates one of the **largest bike-sharing services in the USA**.

Predicting bike demand helps in:

- Planning bicycle availability  
- Managing stations  
- Allocating maintenance personnel  

Factors affecting demand include:

- Season  
- Humidity  
- Rainfall  
- Weekdays  
- Holidays  

The goal is to **build a predictive model** to estimate bike demand based on these factors.

---

### 📞 Project 3: Comcast Telecom Consumer Complaints Analysis

#### Description

Comcast is a **global telecommunications company** that has received numerous customer complaints about poor service.

In **October 2016**, the company was fined **$2.3 million** after over **1000 consumer complaints**.

---

#### Objective

Analyze the complaint dataset to:

- Identify common issues faced by customers  
- Understand complaint patterns  
- Highlight major service problems  
- Suggest areas of improvement
