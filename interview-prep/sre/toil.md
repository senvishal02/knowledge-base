# Toil & Automation

> **Toil elimination and automation strategy questions.**

!!! question "Q: How do you identify and measure toil?"
    Toil is work that is manual, repetitive, automatable, tactical, lacking enduring value, and scales linearly with service growth. Measure by tracking time spent on operational tasks vs engineering work. Target: <50% toil.
