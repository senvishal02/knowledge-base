# On-call & Incident Management

> **Incident management and on-call questions.**

!!! question "Q: Walk through your incident response process"
    1. **Detect** — Monitoring alerts, customer reports
    2. **Triage** — Severity assessment (P1-P4)
    3. **Mitigate** — Stop the bleeding (rollback, scale, failover)
    4. **Resolve** — Fix the root cause
    5. **Post-mortem** — Blameless review, action items, prevention
