# MLOps Concepts

> **Core MLOps concepts and lifecycle questions.**

!!! question "Q: What is MLOps and why is it important?"
    MLOps applies DevOps principles to machine learning systems. It addresses the unique challenges of ML: data dependencies, model versioning, experiment tracking, feature management, model drift, and reproducibility. Without MLOps, ML projects fail to reach or stay in production.
