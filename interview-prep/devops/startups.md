# Startup DevOps Interview Guide

> **Startup-specific DevOps interview preparation.**

## What Startups Look For

- **Breadth over depth** — ability to work across the entire stack
- **Speed of delivery** — shipping features fast without sacrificing reliability
- **Cost consciousness** — optimising cloud spend from day one
- **Hands-on mentality** — no "that is not my job" attitude
- **Automation first** — eliminating manual work proactively
