# Behavioral Interview Questions

> **Behavioral questions for senior DevOps roles — use STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).**

!!! question "Q: Tell me about a time you handled a major production incident"
    **STAR Framework:**

    - **Situation:** Describe the incident (severity, impact, services affected)
    - **Task:** Your role and responsibility during the incident
    - **Action:** Specific steps you took (diagnosis, mitigation, communication)
    - **Result:** Resolution, post-mortem findings, preventive measures implemented

!!! question "Q: How do you handle disagreements with team members on technical decisions?"
    Focus on: data-driven decision making, prototyping, ADRs, and respectful debate.

---

*Prepare 3-5 strong STAR stories before each interview.*
