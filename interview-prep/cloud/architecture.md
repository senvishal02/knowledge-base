# Multi-cloud & Architecture

> **Multi-cloud architecture and design questions.**

!!! question "Q: When would you recommend a multi-cloud strategy?"
    Multi-cloud is justified when:
    
    - Avoiding vendor lock-in is a business requirement
    - Regulatory compliance requires data in specific regions
    - Leveraging best-of-breed services from different providers
    - Disaster recovery across providers
    
    However: it adds complexity, cost, and requires abstraction layers.
